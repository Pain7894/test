About SFE and EIT In 2022
Attention teachers, students and parents! According to the order of the Ministry of Education and Science from March 23, 2022, students of 4th and 9th grades will be released from the SFE in 2022. The Ministry of Education and Science is also discussing the possibility of abolishing the EIT, the SFE and the only entrance exam to the EIT for 11th grade graduates. As soon as the official decision is made, the administration of the educational institution will inform the teachers, students and parents of the graduates
